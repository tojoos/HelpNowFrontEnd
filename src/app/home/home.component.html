Our History

Founding and Early Years

HelpNow Fundraise was founded in 2022 by a student friends, Lisa and Bob. The two had a vision of creating an innovative way to raise money for causes they believed in, and HelpNow was their answer. The company quickly grew in popularity and helped to raise millions of dollars for charities, schools, and other good causes over the next four years. In 2012, HelpNow was named by Forbes Magazine as one of the top 10 most innovative fundraising companies in the world.

2020

Expansion and Growth

In 2013, HelpNow took their fundraising concept to the next level by introducing a new online platform. This allowed people to easily create campaigns, share information, and even donate money to their favorite causes. Over the next two years, the company continued to grow and expand. They added new features to their platform, opened offices in new countries, and began working with some of the world’s largest charities.

2021

Continued Success

HelpNow Fundraise continued to experience success throughout 2016 and 2017. They opened new offices in the US, Europe, and Asia, and their online platform and fundraising capabilities grew even more. In 2018, HelpNow was named by Inc Magazine as one of the fastest growing companies in the US.

2021

Global Expansion

In 2019, HelpNow began to expand their services globally. They opened new offices in Africa, Asia, and Latin America and started to work with some of the world’s largest charities. Today, HelpNow is one of the leading fundraising companies in the world. They continue to innovate, grow, and help to raise millions of dollars for good causes around the world.
